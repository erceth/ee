About Me

Hello, my name is Eric Ethington. Welcome to my website. This site serves as a front page of ericethington.com. The purpose of ericethington.com is two parts. One, a place for me to show off my personal projects and two, to help people get to know me a little better. From now on every time I create a new personal project I will put a link here for people to refer to.

I’m a web developer. I am graduated in Computer Science from Brigham Young University in April 2013. I have been working as a front end web developer ever since. While in college I had an internship for a year where I got my taste for web development and decided that’s that I wanted as a career.

I also have a strong interest in Psychology. I graduated with a minor simply because I liked it. I read psychology type book often and I am fascinated with how people work.

I have a smart and beautiful wife that I love very much. We are best friends. We love spending time with each other and sharing our big ideas with each other.

I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. Members of the church are often called Mormons because of their belief of additional scripture called The Book of Mormon. Some of the interesting practices that set us apart are no drinking alcohol, no drugs, no sex before marriage and very strong family values. A significant percentage of Mormons speak a different language. Many Mormons are very successful in business.

That’s a little bit about me. I hope you find some interest in me an my projects.
